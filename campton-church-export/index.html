Located in the foothills of the White mountains, Campton Baptist Church (CBC), is home to a friendly group of Christian believers who will make you feel at home. In fact, we think of ourselves as a family - a family that honors God and prioritizes personal faith. We meet together in our historic buildings for worship and praise; and for instruction and counsel from the Scriptures; for fellowship, and for furthering the reach and impact of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Pastor What We Believe

We gather every Sunday at 10am

Our main weekly service meets at 10 AM, Sundays. Expect a blend of traditional and contemporary music, public reading of Scriptures, prayers, recitation of the Apostle’s Creed and/or the Lord’s Prayer, followed by a biblically-themed sermon from our pastor. After services we invite everyone for brunch in our fellowship hall.
