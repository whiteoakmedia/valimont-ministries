Our Pastor

Pastor Charles Green

Pastor Chuck is CBC’s preaching/teaching pastor. Expect to hear from him energetic, biblical messages that are thoughtful and thought-provoking. He relates: “The Bible is filled with the greatest stories and story ever told, and contains the greatest message ever heard or received. As a communicator of that extraordinary content, I strive to measure up to its excellence,” Pastor Chuck is a graduate of Southeastern University (BA) and Liberty Theological Seminary (MA, MDiv, ThM). He is a veteran (USAF), a published writer, a fair golfer and a proud father and grandfather.
