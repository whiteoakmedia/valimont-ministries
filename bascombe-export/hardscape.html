What is Hardscape?

Hardscaping is the art of incorporating non-living elements into your landscape design. This includes features such as patios, walkways, retaining walls, fire pits, water features, and more. Unlike softscapes (plants and vegetation), hardscapes provide structural elements that can greatly enhance the aesthetics and functionality of your outdoor spaces.
