Prayer

Hope Church embraces Spirit Empowered prayer (Zech 4:6) We encourage, model and teach corporate and personal growth in prayer. “Call upon me in the day of trouble, and I will rescue you…” This is the promise of our Heavenly Father, (Psalm 50:15, Jeremiah 33:3). Hope Church seeks God’s will to be done on earth as it is in heaven as we call upon him in prayer. Believing prayer is the DNA of every ministry at Hope. If you have a prayer request, please send it along to us and we will believe with you for your miracle.
