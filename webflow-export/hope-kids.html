A service just for kids!

Children’s Ministry is an exciting part of what God is doing here at Hope! Each Sunday offers ministry for the children. The children stay in the adult service until after the offering time, then they are dismissed for their own service designed specifically for them. This includes teaching, games, activities and crafts that are designed to reinforce the Bible lesson of the day. We encourage everyone with children to take advantage of this dynamic ministry.

Helping Kids Dive Deeper into the Bible's Truths

​Bible Engagement takes kids into a deeper understanding of what the Bible teaches. Elementary aged kids are endlessly curious, so make sure you satisfy their curiosity about the Bible, too! Kids can understand a lot more than they get credit for, and that’s why Learn Kids gives them an age-appropriate understanding of Bible truths such as who God is and how He helps us overcome so they know what each truth means and how it impacts their lives.

Our Curriculum
